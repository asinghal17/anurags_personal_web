Landing Page Design and Implementation

MOODI: Dress Your Style

TEAM: Anurag Singhal, Adi Gulati, Mutahhar Sajjad, Sahil Doshi

MOODI is a clothing rental subscription service that allows you to check out trendy, upscale clothes every month. We've all experienced the moment when we realize there's nothing exciting left in the closet or that we've been wearing the same item over and over again. Moodi helps to ease that pain by allowing you to check out items monthly, and keep your closet looking fresh.


Worked with team to Design, Develop, and Implement a Creative Landing Page.
